The IFINIX Suite

Core-Connected Solutions for Every Layer of Banking Operations

The IFINIX suite is a set of four connected solutions addressing workflow automation, financial reconciliation, real-time payment switching, and data intelligence. Each product is powerful on its own. Together, they form the operational layer that modern financial institutions need.

Core Platform | Flagship Product

IWE | Infinity Workflow Engine

The Operational Engine That Powers Every Process Across Your Bank

IWE is the operational heart of IFINIX. It is a low-code workflow automation platform purpose-built for banking and financial services. IWE transforms manual, paper-driven processes into structured, automated, and fully auditable digital workflows from customer onboarding and loan origination to compliance management and back-office processing. Every process becomes visible, measurable, and controllable.

What IWE Does

End-to-End Process Automation

Transforms manual, multi-step banking processes into structured digital workflows with automatic task routing, parallel processing, and SLA enforcement.

Intelligent Task Management

Assigns, tracks, and escalates work items across teams and departments with priority-based queuing and real-time visibility into every active task.

Configurable Approval Workflows

Multi-level, role-based approval chains that adapt to product type, transaction value, risk category, and institutional policy, all without code changes.

Exception Management

Captures, routes, and resolves process exceptions through structured workflows instead of email threads and spreadsheets. Every exception is tracked and auditable.

Full Audit Trail & Compliance Reporting

Every action, decision, and state change is logged with timestamps, user attribution, and contextual data, providing a complete compliance record for regulators and auditors.

Business-Configurable, Not IT-Dependent

Business operations teams design, modify, and deploy workflow changes through a visual configuration environment without requiring software development or IT intervention.

IWE Core Capability Groups

Workflow Automation

Process Design Studio for visual workflow creation, dynamic task routing, parallel processing, conditional branching, and SLA management with automated escalation.

Business Rules Engine

Dynamic approval thresholds, automated escalation policies, configurable business rules, and policy enforcement that adapts to your institution's operational requirements.

Document Management

Centralised, versioned, access-controlled document repository integrated directly into workflows. Upload, review, approve, and archive documents within the process.

Intelligent Document Processing

OCR-powered data extraction from scanned documents, automated classification, and structured data capture that eliminates manual data entry from paper-based processes.

Integration Framework

REST APIs, SOAP web services, and pre-built system connectors that link IWE to core banking, CRM, compliance, and third-party systems across the institution.

Reporting & Analytics

Real-time operational dashboards, KPI monitoring, process performance analytics, and custom reporting that gives management visibility into every operational metric.

Security & Governance

Role-based access control (RBAC), single sign-on (SSO), comprehensive audit trail, data encryption, and institutional governance policies enforced at the platform level.

Key Operational Areas

Customer Onboarding & KYC

Structured workflows for identity verification, document collection, risk assessment, and regulatory checks, reducing onboarding time from days to hours.

Loan Origination

End-to-end loan processing from application capture through credit assessment, approval routing, documentation, and disbursement, all within a single automated workflow.

Account Opening

Digital account opening workflows that coordinate data capture, verification, approval, and system activation across departments and core banking platforms.

Compliance Management

Regulatory reporting workflows, policy attestation, compliance monitoring, and audit preparation , structured and automated to reduce risk and manual overhead.

Trade Finance

Letters of credit, bank guarantees, documentary collections, and trade settlement workflows automated with multi-party coordination and compliance checks.

Internal Approvals

Budget approvals, capital expenditure requests, policy changes, and operational decisions routed through configurable approval chains with full audit visibility.

Procurement

Vendor onboarding, purchase requisitions, quotation management, and procurement approvals automated through structured workflows with policy enforcement.

HR Operations

Employee onboarding, leave management, performance reviews, and HR service requests processed through standardised, auditable workflows.

Case Management

Customer complaints, disputes, fraud investigations, and operational issues tracked through structured case workflows with SLA enforcement and resolution tracking.

Service Requests

Internal and customer service requests captured, categorised, routed, and resolved through automated workflows with real-time status tracking.

Back-Office Operations

Reconciliation triggers, settlement processing, report generation, and operational maintenance tasks automated to reduce manual dependency across back-office teams.

Platform Architecture

Three Layers. One Operational Experience.

IWE is built on a three-layer architecture that separates user experience, integration, and execution, giving institutions flexibility without complexity.

IFINIX One

The Unified Dashboard. A single interface where every user, from front-line staff to senior management, accesses their tasks, monitors operational performance, and takes action. Role-based views ensure each user sees exactly what they need.

IFINIX Connect

The Integration Layer. Pre-built connectors and open APIs that link IWE to core banking systems, CRM platforms, compliance tools, and third-party services. Data flows seamlessly between systems without manual intervention.

IFINIX Flow

The Execution Engine. The workflow runtime that orchestrates task routing, business rule evaluation, SLA management, escalation, and process completion. The engine that turns workflow designs into operational reality.

Technology

Built on Modern, Enterprise-Grade Technology

IWE is built on a proven technology stack designed for performance, security, and scalability in enterprise banking environments.

Frontend

React 17 with React Router 6 for dynamic single-page application navigation. Bootstrap 5 and MUI v5 for responsive, accessible UI components. Modular component architecture for maintainability.

Backend

Java 21 with Spring Boot 3.4.5 providing a robust, high-performance server framework. RESTful API architecture with comprehensive service layer and business logic orchestration.

Database

Oracle database for enterprise-grade data management, transactional integrity, and scalability. Optimised schema design for high-volume workflow and audit data.

Security

OAuth2 authentication, LDAP integration, role-based access control (RBAC), and JWT RS256 token-based authorisation. Enterprise security standards enforced at every layer.

Deployment

Apache Tomcat for development environments, Oracle WebLogic for production. AWS Amplify for cloud-hosted deployments. Flexible deployment models to match institutional infrastructure.

Financial Integrity Solution

iRec | Intelligent Reconciliation

Accurate. Automated. Auditable.

iRec automates reconciliation across accounts, systems, and ledgers, reducing the time, cost, and human error associated with one of banking's most critical and labour-intensive processes. From multi-source matching to structured exception resolution, iRec ensures financial integrity at every level.

Automated Multi-Source Matching

Configurable matching rules that reconcile transactions across multiple data sources, accounts, and ledger systems automatically, handling one-to-one, one-to-many, and many-to-many scenarios.

Real-Time Exception Identification

Unmatched and discrepant items are flagged immediately with contextual information, reducing the time between occurrence and detection from days to seconds.

Structured Exception Resolution

Exception items flow into structured resolution workflows with assignment, escalation, and tracking, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks and every item reaches closure.

Regulatory Reporting

Pre-built and configurable reconciliation reports for internal audit, external audit, and regulatory submission. Complete audit trail of every match, exception, and resolution action.

Integration with IWE

Reconciliation exceptions that require operational action are routed directly into IWE workflows , connecting financial integrity with operational execution in a single platform.

Payment Connectivity Solution

iSwitch | Interbank Switching

Real-Time Interbank Transactions. Compliant. Reliable.

iSwitch enables banks to participate in CEFT and interbank switching networks with confidence and operational precision. Built around banking compliance, operational control, and transaction-level reliability, iSwitch handles high-volume real-time payment processing with full monitoring and exception management.

CEFT Connectivity

Direct integration with Sri Lanka's Common Electronic Fund Transfer (CEFT) network, enabling real-time interbank fund transfers with full protocol compliance and message validation.

High-Volume Processing

Engineered for throughput. iSwitch processes high volumes of concurrent transactions with sub-second response times, queuing, and load management for peak periods.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live transaction dashboards, throughput monitoring, and alerting that gives operations teams full visibility into payment flow health and performance metrics in real time.

Compliance & Standards

Built-in compliance with central bank regulations, message format standards, and audit requirements. Every transaction is logged with full traceability for regulatory review.

Exception Management

Failed, timed-out, and rejected transactions are captured, categorised, and routed through structured resolution workflows, integrated with IWE for operational follow-through.

Operational Intelligence Solution

Data Platform | Warehouse & Analytics

Your Bank Generates Data Every Second. Start Using It.

The IFINIX Data Platform consolidates transactional and operational data from across the institution into a unified warehouse and delivers analytics that turn raw data into actionable decisions. Operational reporting, customer intelligence, and predictive insight from one connected foundation.

Unified Data Consolidation

Aggregates data from core banking, IWE, iRec, iSwitch, and external sources into a single, consistent data warehouse, eliminating silos and providing a single source of truth.

Operational Analytics

Real-time and historical analytics on process performance, throughput, SLA compliance, and operational efficiency across every workflow and department in the institution.

Customer Intelligence

Behavioural analytics, product usage patterns, and customer segmentation that help institutions understand their customers and make data-driven decisions about products and services.

Regulatory Reporting

Automated generation of regulatory reports from consolidated data. Consistent, accurate, and auditable reporting that reduces the manual effort and risk of compliance submissions.

Predictive Insight

Trend analysis, anomaly detection, and predictive models that help institutions anticipate operational bottlenecks, customer behaviour shifts, and risk events before they materialise.
