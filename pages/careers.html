We Are Looking for People Who Think Like Bankers and Build Like Engineers

The work requires deep understanding of financial institutions plus technical capability. We are growing. The problems are real and consequential.

What We Offer

Work That Is Worth Your Expertise

At IFINIX, domain expertise is the foundation. We build technology for banking operations because we understand banking operations. Every product decision, every client engagement, and every team member reflects that principle. Your knowledge of the domain is not a background qualification here. It is the primary asset.

Embedded Consulting

Work directly with banking operations teams as an embedded consulting partner. You will be in the room where decisions are made, not building from second-hand specifications.

Problem-Driven Design

The problem drives the solution and domain knowledge drives the design. We do not build features for the sake of features. Every line of work traces back to a real operational need.

Full Engagement Visibility

Understand the full engagement and contribute beyond your immediate scope. We value people who see the whole picture and take initiative where it matters.

Shape What We Become

We are early enough that you can grow with it. The processes, the culture, the product direction are still being shaped. Your contribution will define what IFINIX becomes.

How We Work

The IFINIX Way of Working

Banking-First Thinking

Every decision starts with the banking operation. We understand the regulatory environment, the operational risks, and the daily realities of financial institutions before we write a single line of code.

Clarity Over Complexity

We value straightforward solutions that people can understand, maintain, and trust. Complexity is not a sign of sophistication. Clarity is.

Ownership and Accountability

When you take on a piece of work, it is yours. We expect people to own outcomes, not just tasks. That means following through, raising issues early, and delivering what you committed to.

Domain-Led Delivery

Delivery is guided by domain understanding, not just project timelines. We ship when the solution genuinely addresses the operational requirement, not when the calendar says so.

Continuous Improvement

We refine how we work as deliberately as we refine what we build. Retrospectives, knowledge sharing, and honest feedback are part of the routine, not special occasions.

Partnership

We treat clients as partners and teammates as collaborators. The best outcomes happen when people work together with mutual respect, shared context, and a common goal.

Current Opportunities

We Are Building a Team of Domain Experts and Execution Specialists

Consulting & Domain Roles

  • Banking Operations Consultants

    Map, redesign, and implement operational improvements across banking institutions. You will work at the intersection of process expertise and technology enablement.

  • Business Analysts (Banking Domain)

    Bridge banking requirements and technology design. Translate complex operational needs into clear, actionable specifications that engineering teams can build against.

  • Pre-Sales and Solution Consultants

    Engage with banking clients at the business requirements level. Understand their operational challenges and demonstrate how IFINIX solutions address them directly.

Technology Roles

  • Solution Architects

    Design technical integration between IWE and banking ecosystems. Define architecture that is robust, scalable, and aligned with operational realities.

  • Software Engineers

    Java and React, banking operations technology. Build the systems that financial institutions rely on for their daily operations.

  • QA Engineers

    Reliability and correctness through structured testing. Ensure that every release meets the standards that banking operations demand.

  • UI/UX Designers

    Operational interfaces that reduce friction. Design experiences for banking professionals who use these tools every day under real operational pressure.

  • AI Engineers

    OCR, intelligent document processing, and decision support. Apply machine learning to real banking operations problems where accuracy is non-negotiable.

  • DevOps Engineers

    Infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and operational reliability. Keep the platform running with the uptime and security that financial institutions expect.

  • Implementation Specialists

    Deliver IWE deployments and manage both the technical and operational sides. You are the bridge between our product and the client's environment.

Product & Leadership Roles

  • Product Managers (Banking Operations)

    Drive product direction based on real operational requirements. You will work closely with banking clients and internal teams to prioritize what matters most.

  • Engagement Managers

    Manage complex banking transformation engagements. Coordinate across consulting, technology, and client stakeholders to deliver successful outcomes.

What to Expect

Genuine Work. Direct Impact.

A typical day at IFINIX involves working on problems that banking professionals face right now. You might be on a call with a client's operations team in the morning, refining a workflow design at midday, and reviewing implementation details in the afternoon. The work is varied because the domain is complex, and we need people who can move across those layers.

Banking Domain Knowledge

We invest in making sure every team member understands the banking context they are building for. Domain training, client exposure, and knowledge sharing are part of how we operate.

Delivery Quality

We hold ourselves to a high standard because our clients depend on what we build. Quality is not a phase. It is built into every step of the process.

Team Capability

We grow people deliberately. Mentorship, cross-functional exposure, and increasing responsibility are how careers develop at IFINIX. We want people who will be here for the long term.

Interested in What We Are Building?

We are building IFINIX with people who understand banking. If you bring domain expertise, technical capability, or both, we want to hear from you. The work is real, the impact is direct, and the team is growing.
